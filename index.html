Meet the Owner: Brittany Alexander

Hi, I'm Brittany the founder of You Missed A Spot Cleaning Solutions. What started in 2019 as a vision to build something of my own has grown into a business rooted in hard work, resilience, and a true passion for helping others. As a mother, a full-time worker, and a woman determined to create a better future, I understand how overwhelming life can get and how much a clean, peaceful space can truly make a difference.

I didn't build this company to just clean homes, I built it to serve people. Every home we enter is treated with care, respect, and attention to detail, because I know what it means to trust someone in your space. My goal is not just to meet expectations, but to exceed them every single time.

When you book with us, you're not just getting a cleaning service, you're getting someone who genuinely cares.
